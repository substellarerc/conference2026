The solar eclipse totallity path

Tordesillas is at the totallity part of the solar eclipse on August 12, 2026. However, on the day of the eclipse, depending on the local weather conditions, we may choose an alternative location to enjoy the celestial spectacle. More on our page (not yet).

Tordesillas

Without a doubt, Tordesillas is one of the municipalities in Valladolid with the most history, where important events such as the signing of the Treaty of Tordesillas and the enclosure of Joanna I of Castile, better known as Joanna "the Mad," took place.

In Tordesillas, you can stroll through the old town, visit a multitude of historic buildings and museums, and enjoy the tranquility of the area.
