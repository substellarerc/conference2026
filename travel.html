Traveling to Tordesillas

Public transportation

By Plane:

You can get to Tordesillas by plane arriving at Valladolid airport or at Madrid airport.

Landing in Valladolid Airport

Valladolid airport has regular connections with Barcelona, Paris, London and Bruxelles, and other seasonal flights.
The best way to reach Tordesillas from Valladolid airport is by taxi (around 62 euros on week-ends and 48 euros in ordinary fare). You can also do Valladolid airport to Valladolid Bus station by bus or taxi and then Valladolid bus station to Tordesillas by bus.

Valladolid airport to Valladolid

Valladolid Airport is 15 Km from the city of Valladolid. Linecar operates a bus service to Valladolid Bus Station. Bus timetable is:
From Valladolid airport to Valladolid Bus Station each 90 minutes form 6:45 am. to 22:50 (from Monday throught Saturday) and from 9:45 to 22:50 (Sunday).
From Valladolid Bus Station to airport each 90 minutes from 6:00 am. to 21:45 (monday throught saturday) and from 9:00 throught 21:45 (Sunday).
Journey time is 20 minutes and cost is €3.00 one way and €5.00 return. You can buy the ticket in the bus. Bus Stop in Valladolid airport is just outside Arrivals, please take a look to the map.
Taxi journay from Valladolid airport to Valladolid center should cost around 22 (ordinary fare) and 24 (week-end fare, Sunday and Saturday from 16:00 h.) euros.

Landing in Madrid

Madrid airport (Barajas) has connections worldwide. From Madrid airport you can reach Madrid by metro or by taxi. Madrid Underground connects you with the center of the city, train and bus stations.
We recommend to reach Tordesillas by bus, but you can also go first to Valladolid (by bus or train) and then from Valladolid to Tordesillas by bus or taxi.

By bus:

Notice that each travel Valladolid-Tordesillas, Valladolid-Madrid and Madrid-Tordesillas is serviced by a different bus company.
In Madrid, buses must be taken in Estacion Sur de Autobuses (metro Stop: Méndez Álvaro, the bus station is part of the same building). The web page of this station is http://www.estaciondeautobuses.com/ .

  • From Madrid to Tordesillas The bus company is Avanza ( avanzabus.com ). There are a few connections daily (it takes between 2h10m and 2h30m). For Sunday 18 September there are the following buses: at 10:30,13:30, 16:00, 17:30 and 22:00. From Monday to Saturday we have the following buses: 10:30, 13:30 and 17:30.
    From Tordesillas to Madrid (Estación Sur de Autobuses) from Monday to Saturday: 10:20, 11:20 and 18:20. On Sunday, 10:20, 11:20, 18:20, 20:00, and 21:20.
  • From Valladolid to Tordesillas: There is a bus station in Tordesillas, with connections to Valladolid and Madrid. If you want to do this travel by bus, you must go to Valladolid bus station (Calle Puente Colgante, close to the train station). The bus company that travels to Tordesillas is "La Regional" (journey take 30 min. around). http://www.laregionalvsa.com. On Sunday 18 september, bus departs are: 08:30, 14:00, 16:30, 18:00, 20:30 and 22:30. You can check timetables at the webpage.
  • From Madrid to Valladolid: The bus company is Alsa ( http://www.alsa.es). In Madrid, buses must be taken in Estacion Sur de Autobuses (metro Stop: Méndez Álvaro). There is nearly a bus each hour. Also some buses depart from Madrid Airport (Barajas, Terminal 4). Tickets can be bought on the web page. (Notice that buses departing from Barajas are in great demand, hence it is recommended to buy tickets in advance).

By train:

We think it is easier to come from Madrid to Tordesillas by bus. However, if you want to come by train, you must go first to Valladolid and then from Valladolid to Tordesillas by bus (there is no train station in Tordesillas), as follows:
Trains between Madrid (Chamartin train station) and Valladolid (Campo Grande station) are very frequent. For detailed timetables you can visit http://www.renfe.es , the official page of the Spanish railway company. Then you get a bus from Valladolid to Tordesillas as explained above. The train station and bus station are about 500 metres one from the other, see map

By Taxi:

Any taxi can carry you to Tordesillas from Valladolid or from Valladolid airport. Prices are approximatively: Valladolid airport - Valladolid 24 euros; Valladolid airport - Tordesillas, 62 euros; Valladolid - Tordesillas, 40 euros.
