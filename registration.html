Preregistration

By completing the form and submitting an abstract, you indicate your interest in attending the conference. Due to the venue's limited capacity, the organizers reserve the right to select attendees based on proposed contributions.

Preregistration opens on December 10 and it will close on February 15.

Selected participants will be notified by March 1, at which time information regarding the final registration and fee payment will be provided. The regular conference fee is expected to be 300 EUR (late registration fee 400 EUR).
